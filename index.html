But make sure to look at their fat and sodium levels to decide for yourself if they're healthy!

According to the American Heart Association, having too much sodium in your diet may increase your chance of having high blood pressure. People with high blood pressure are more likely to develop heart disease or have a stroke.

Our Live Well? menu features items that are full of Louisiana flavor, all only 350 calories or less. But our listed calories have many discreprencies, so enjoy eating food without knowing exactly what you're putting in your body!
